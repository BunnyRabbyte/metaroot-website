This Privacy Policy explains how MetaRoot Technologies Ltd (“MetaRoot”, “we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses and protects information when you use our website metaroot.tech and any future apps or services we provide.

MetaRoot Technologies Ltd is a technology company focused on building work-first mobile apps, smart connectivity tools and partner integrations. You can contact us at metaroottech@outlook.com.

We use the information we collect to:

Depending on your location, we may rely on different legal bases including:

We keep your information only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. For example, if you email us, we may keep that email for record-keeping and follow-up.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights such as:

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect the information we hold. However, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the internet.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to third-party websites or services. Their privacy practices are separate from ours, and we encourage you to review their policies before providing any information.

10. Children’s Privacy

MetaRoot does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.
