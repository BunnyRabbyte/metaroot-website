Company Objective

To become the core layer that connects workers, platforms, telcos and financial systems – starting from Africa, built for global scale. One ecosystem, many specialised apps.

Who is MetaRoot?

MetaRoot Technologies Ltd is a product studio and technology company focused on the “roots” of everyday digital life – connectivity, data, payments and simple tools that help real people do real work.

We are not just building one app. MetaRoot is an ecosystem of services that can be branded, white-labelled and integrated with partners who serve companies, merchants, communities and new kinds of digital workers across Africa and beyond.

What We Build

Our work is organised around a few clear pillars:

Work-First Mobile Apps

Tools that help companies, riders, traders and field workers see their earnings, costs and opportunities clearly – not just move from trip to trip.

Smart Connectivity & Data

Partnerships with telcos and ISPs to create bundles that prioritise high-value apps: maps, ride-hailing, chat, finance and verified work tools.

APIs & White-Label Systems

Infrastructure that allows fleets, cooperatives, fintechs and telcos to launch their own branded experiences powered by MetaRoot in the background.

Who We Work With

Parastatals & Field Workers

People whose income depends on movement, connectivity and clear information.

Telcos & ISPs

Network operators looking for focused, work-centric data products and insights.

Fintech & Digital Platforms

Partners building credit, savings, insurance and marketplaces around real behaviour.
