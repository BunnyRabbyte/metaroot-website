1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, phone or tablet) when you visit a website. They help the website recognise your device and remember information about your visit.

2. How We Use Cookies

At this early stage, MetaRoot may use only basic cookies, for example to:

  • Keep the website running securely and reliably.
  • Understand simple usage patterns (such as which pages are visited) if we enable analytics.

As our products evolve, we may introduce additional cookies to support login, preferences or integrations. This policy will be updated if that happens.

3. Types of Cookies We May Use

  • Strictly necessary cookies – required for basic security and core functionality.
  • Analytics cookies – to help us understand how visitors use our website so we can improve content and performance.

4. Third-Party Cookies

If we use third-party services (for example, analytics tools or embedded content), those providers may place cookies on your device. Their use of cookies is governed by their own privacy and cookie policies.

5. Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to manage or delete cookies through their settings. You can usually:

  • Block all cookies.
  • Block cookies from specific sites.
  • Delete existing cookies.

If you choose to block certain cookies, some features of our website may not function correctly.
